Our Experience

We had the privilege to participate in the Erasmus project in Spain, an experience that deeply impacted our perspectives on learning and personal development, especially in the field of informatics. This opportunity immersed us in Spanish culture in a profound and authentic manner. During our practical training with our teacher Antonio, we engaged with advanced programming concepts and worked on real-world IT projects. The experience provided invaluable lessons, both professionally and personally, as we collaborated with skilled professionals and gained insights into cutting-edge technologies.

In addition to our IT-focused activities, we explored vibrant cities such as Granada, Malaga, Cordoba, Seville, and Cordoba. Each city offered rich cultural and historical experiences, complementing our technical learning with a deep appreciation for Spain's diverse heritage. We connected with locals who generously shared their traditions and customs, broadening our cultural understanding and enriching our global perspective.

Participating in IT courses and educational workshops equipped us with essential skills for the digital era, preparing us for future careers in technology. Beyond technical skills, we engaged in traditional cultural events like flamenco evenings, which enhanced our understanding of Spanish artistry and human diversity.

This transformative experience fostered our independence, responsibility, and communication skills, while nurturing a collaborative spirit essential for professional growth. We are grateful for the opportunity to explore and learn in a country renowned for its cultural richness, leaving us with indelible memories and a lasting curiosity to explore more of the world around us.
